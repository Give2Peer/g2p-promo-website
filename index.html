Give2Peer

An item location and donation service.

Let's adapt the economics of sharing to the information era!
Stuff should be really easy to donate and receive for free.
Lost items should be easy to find again.

Subscribe for news »

Server-Side

The server's specifications are written in Gherkin, and its API is really minimal. The idea is to allow clients to choose between servers, so that big institutions may use the service privately. An implementation in PHP/pgSQL, as a Symfony2 bundle, is currently being worked on.

Client-Side

An Android app is under works, and should be followed by a FirefoxOs app, a Chrome app, and a simple website. We will also port our Android app to Blackberry devices. Our current team of unpaid geeks lacks the hardware required for an iPhone/iPad app.

Hackathons!

If you are interested in organizing or participating in peer-programming hackathons for the benefit of peer giving, write to hackathon@give2peer.org.
They're happening pretty much every week-end. Even if you do not code, there are lots of other things to do that help.
